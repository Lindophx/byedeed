Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I get a fair price for my home?
Our offers are based on current market conditions, the property's condition, and comparable sales in the area. While cash offers are typically below full retail price, many sellers find the net amount is comparable when you factor in saved commissions, repairs, carrying costs, and closing costs — plus the certainty and speed.
Do I have to pay any fees or commissions?
Absolutely not. There are no agent commissions, no closing fees, and no hidden costs charged to you. The offer we make is the amount you walk away with.
How fast can you really close?
Because we're cash buyers, we don't need bank approval or appraisals. In most cases we can close in as little as 7 days. If you need more time, we can work with that too.
Do I need to clean or repair the house first?
Not at all. We buy homes completely as-is. Leave behind what you don't want — furniture, junk, you name it. We'll handle it.
What if my home is in foreclosure or I'm behind on payments?
This is one of our specialties. We can often move fast enough to help you avoid a foreclosure on your record. The sooner you reach out, the more options you'll have — so don't wait.
Do you buy homes outside of Arizona?
Yes! While Arizona is our home base, we're actively expanding to high-demand markets nationwide. Give us a call and let's talk — if we can't help directly, we may be able to connect you with a trusted buyer in your area.
